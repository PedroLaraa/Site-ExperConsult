ExperConsult

Founded over 20 years ago, ExperConsult Engenharia began its operations with a focus on providing advisory services for legal approvals to federal, state, and municipal agencies throughout Brazil.

Thus, a company emerged that, from its inception, would be marked by competence and professionalism.

Starting with the development of industrial layouts and legal approvals, the company has evolved, creating specialized Architecture and Engineering departments to serve clients with all the necessary disciplines for project release.

Our Structure

The company's headquarters is situated in the city of Igarapé, Minas Gerais. Its founder and current director is food engineer João Batista de Oliveira Demenek, who brings extensive prior experience in the field, including his tenure as director of meatpacking industries in the state of Minas Gerais.
